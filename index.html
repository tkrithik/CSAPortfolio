From hackathons to competitions, my projects have always been designed and focused on making the world a better place than it already is. Using my computer science knowledge and growing skill set, I am to change the world with positive impacts.

About me

In our growing world, technology is the basis for all of our successes. Looking around, everybody and everything uses some form of technology. In order to make the world better, I, Krithik Tamilvanan, dream to be involved too.

About Me

From a young age, I have been deeply interested in coding and the world of computer science. With the encouragement of my family, friends, and teachers, I pursued computer science as not only a career but also a dream. Computer science lets me express myself through projects for many causes. Whether it is helping patients or driving cars, computer science always has a key role. Utilizing my growing skill set, I plan to major in computer science and use my knowledge to help change our world.

Experiences

In my years of high school, I have developed a love for computer science, as shown through my many achievements. Through my participation in Destination Imagination, a STEAM-based extracurricular activity, I have incorporated my skills in many ways. For example, I have built and programmed a drone completely from scratch, through the use of motors, electronic speed controllers, wooden frames, and more components. I have also created flowers that replicate the opening and closing of actual flowers. These flowers tilt towards the sun and can bloom and close as well. Together, with the skills I have cultivated, I feel I can accomplish my dream and change the world.

Having gained a wide range of skills in the computer science world has helped me accomplish many tasks and projects over the years.
